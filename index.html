Top three activities to do in Vancouver

Suspension Bridge

The Capilano Suspension Bridge is a simple suspension bridge crossing the Capilano River in the District of North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The current bridge is 140 metres long and 70 metres above the river.

Night Life

Vancouver's nightlife is among the best in Canada, the best on the western side of North America and maybe even among the best on the planet.

Food

The cultural diversity results in the multifaceted and vibrant food scene in the city. It allows Vancouverites and tourist alike to experience the finest flavours in the world.
